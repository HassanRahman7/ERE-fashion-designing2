BERG FASHION LIBRARY

Berg Fashion Library is an online database of the Bloomsbury Fashion Central portal that offers fully cross-searchable access to an expanding range of Berg content collections – including the Berg Encyclopedia of World Dress and Fashion online, e-books, reference works, images, and much more. It is interdisciplinary in its coverage – besides fashion and textiles it covers disciplines such as anthropology, art history, history, sociology, geography, folklore, museum studies, theater and cultural studies.
