OPAC

An OPAC is a searchable database of a library's collection, including books, journals, theses, and other materials. (OPAC) is a digital database that lists the holdings of a library or group of libraries, allowing users to search for and locate books, journals, and other materials. It's essentially an online library catalog that's accessible to the public, replacing traditional card catalogs. Users can access the OPAC online and search for items by title, author, subject, keyword, or other criteria.
